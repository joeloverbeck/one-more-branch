# Choice Icon Generation Prompts

## Global Style Specification

All 31 icons follow the **Dark Fantasy Emblem** style:

- **Shape**: Circular medallion
- **Background**: Near-black (#1a1a2e)
- **Line art**: Gold (#d4af37), 2-3px stroke weight
- **Detail**: Subtle inner ring/border decoration
- **Size**: 128x128px PNG, transparent outside the circle
- **No text** in the icon
- **Aesthetic**: Ornate, hand-engraved feel; evokes a wax seal or heraldic token

Prepend the following to every prompt below:

> Dark fantasy emblem icon. Circular medallion on near-black (#1a1a2e) background. Gold (#d4af37) line art, 2-3px stroke. Subtle ornate inner ring decoration. 128x128px, transparent outside circle. No text. Hand-engraved heraldic style.
